# GSD — Get Sh*t Done
## Introduction Session Guide

---

## 1. Talking Points / Session Script

**Opening (2 min)**

> "Today I want to show you something we've built to replace the spreadsheets and manual tracking we've been relying on for managing queue work. It's called GSD — Get Sh*t Done — and the whole idea is to make it as easy as possible to see what needs doing, pick it up, and get it closed out."

Key messages to land:
- This is **built for us** — it pulls directly from our actual queues (UK, DE, Transfers)
- It replaces manual lists and chasing conversations
- Managers can see everything in one place; agents see exactly what they need to action

---

**The problem we're solving (2 min)**

> "Right now, when errors and bookings come in, it can be hard to know: what's outstanding, who's working on what, and whether anything is about to fall through the cracks. GSD gives us a live view of all of that."

Pain points it addresses:
- No visibility on who has picked up what
- Hard to spot blocked or escalated tasks before they become problems
- No easy way to report on what the team completed in a day

---

**What it does — the three-minute tour (5–8 min)**

Walk through each section:

**Dashboard**
> "This is the home screen. You can see at a glance: how many tasks are outstanding, how many are assigned, and a breakdown by queue. If something's on fire — blocked, escalated — it shows up immediately."

**Queue View**
> "Click into any queue and you see every task. You can filter by status — Pending, In Progress, Attention — or use 'Mine' to see just your tasks. Managers also get an 'Unassigned' filter to quickly spot anything that hasn't been picked up yet."

**Task Detail**
> "Click a row and the detail panel opens on the right. From here you can update the status, add notes, delegate to a specific agent, and mark it done. Everything is tracked — who changed what, and when."

**Daily Summary**
> "At the end of the day, or any point during it, the Summary tab shows you a full picture: how many tasks were completed, by whom, by queue, who's currently carrying the most work. It's the basis for your daily standup or end-of-day report."

---

**For managers specifically (3 min)**

> "There are a few things built specifically for you."

- **Assign & delegate** — you can assign tasks to any agent directly from the detail panel, or bulk-assign across multiple rows at once
- **Unassigned filter** — red chip in the queue view shows everything with no owner; this is your 'at risk' view
- **Custom fields** — if you need to track something specific to a queue (e.g. 'Was this booking cancelled?' or 'Flagged with Finance?'), you can add those columns yourself without needing a developer
- **Daily Summary** — team workload section shows live agent task counts with a breakdown by status

---

**What it isn't (1 min)**

> "Just to set expectations — GSD is a task management layer on top of our existing systems. It doesn't replace CHIPS or any other operational tool. It's the coordination layer — who's doing what, and is it done."

## 2. Live Demo Script

**Recommended flow (5–7 minutes of clicking)**

### Step 1 — Dashboard
- Open the app, land on Dashboard
- Point out the stat cards: Outstanding, Assigned, In Progress, Blocked, Escalated, Errors
- **Say:** "This tells us the health of the queue at this moment."
- Point out the Outstanding by Queue table — show how many are assigned vs. total per queue

### Step 2 — Queue View (UK Bookings)
- Click into UK Bookings queue
- **Say:** "Every row is a task. The columns are pulled directly from our data."
- Click the **Unassigned** filter chip
- **Say:** "This is what managers will check first — anything without an owner is at risk."
- Click back to **All**, then click a row to open the detail panel
- **Say:** "This is where agents will spend most of their time."

### Step 3 — Task Detail Panel
- Show the status dropdown — cycle to "In Progress"
- Show the Delegate button — point out agent list
- Add a note in the Notes field
- **Say:** "Every change is tracked. You can see exactly who did what."
- Show any custom fields on the queue if present

### Step 4 — Bulk Actions
- Click the **☐ Select** button
- Tick 3–4 rows
- Show the bulk bar: Assign, Change Status, Archive
- **Say:** "For anything repetitive — assigning a batch of tasks to one agent, or closing out a group of resolved items — this saves a lot of time."

### Step 5 — Daily Summary
- Click the Summary tab
- Point at the stat cards: Completed, In Progress, Blocked, Escalated
- Point at the Completions by Agent chart
- **Say:** "This is your end-of-day view. Who completed what, and across which queues."
- Scroll down to Team Workload
- **Say:** "This is live — it updates as agents pick up and close tasks throughout the day."

### GSD — Get Sh*t Done | Quick Reference

**Your daily flow**

| Step | What to do |
|------|------------|
| 1. Start of day | Go to your queue → click **👤 Mine** to see your tasks |
| 2. Pick up a task | Click a row → set status to **In Progress** |
| 3. Hit a problem? | Set status to **Blocked** or **Escalated** and add a note |
| 4. Task done | Set status to **Done** — it moves to the archive automatically |
| 5. End of day | Check nothing is still **In Progress** — hand over or update |

---

**Status guide**

| Status | Means |
|--------|-------|
| Pending | Not picked up yet |
| In Progress | Someone is working on it |
| Blocked | Waiting on something — needs a note |
| Escalated | Passed to next level |
| Done | Completed and archived |

---

**Tips**

- Use **⚠ Attention** filter to find anything blocked or escalated that needs action
- Add notes when you change status — it helps the next person (and your manager)
- If a task needs someone else, use the **Delegate** button in the detail panel
- Your manager can see your workload in the Summary tab — keep your statuses up to date
